SFU STUDENT GUIDE

CONTEXT: Graphic & Layout Design

TYPE: Personal Work

ROLE: Personal Work

Description

The SFU Student Guide( Red book ) is a booklet compiled and published under the name of Cloud Vision Media & Technology. It’s 2000 copies mainly aims at introducing Vancouver and Simon Fraser University to the international student from China and provide relevant information that will enable them to address the problem encountered in their oversea life.

Tools

Photoshop / Illustrator

Skills

Grapic Design / AI Design / Layout / Publishing

Design Process

This booklet collects some relevant articles which were posted under the name of Cloud Life Daily on the social media platform called “WeChat”. In order to control the number of page and reform articles into the form of book, all articles were re-edited and simplified in the beginning. To avoid insipid reading experience and misunderstanding that user might have when they read this booklet, I applied several layouts to compose text and images but keep those chapters with same content in an unified and tidy style.
