MEV-01

CONTEXT: Game design

TYPE: School Project

ROLE: Programmer

Description

MEV-01 is a cooperative stealth game that has two players utilizing their own skills to inﬁltrate and look for evidence of a vaccine.Both players have their own unique skills that can get each other through the heavily and they need each other in order to progress through undetected successfully. This project is completed under the cooperation of five team members. Instructor from Simon Fraser University and Game developers from EA Game Studio provided many useful advices and feedbacks to help us to improve this game.

Tools

Maya 3D / Illustrator / Java / Unity

Skills

Game Design / GUI design / Java Programming / User test /3D animation

Design Process

Stage 1: Concept Ideation

From the very beginning to the beta version, this project took our five month to design, develop, test and release. In the first phase, we produced a design document that could help our team member to understand their role and some initial game concepts such as playtype, game play, mechanics and so on.

Initial game concepts

  • Core Objective: Player must steal information for his corporation, so he must trespass each room while avoiding guards and hacking devices.
  • Player Type: As the player must solve puzzles by planning and executing action sequences. This game would be more oriented towards the Achiever player type.
  • Gameplay: Player stealth game with an orthographic top-down view of the environment and its ingameobjects, player will be able to move around the environment while constantly entering x-ray mode.
  • Mechanics: General core mechanics are movement via the controller movement sticks, performing hacks on devices and lights at a safe location so that new areas and opportunities are open to the player.

Stage 2: Game world design

Majority of our levels will be indoors in an office, laboratory, and a server room. The offices aesthetics will follow a contemporary office space with kitchens, board rooms, closets, private meeting rooms, and open spaces. Players will be utilizing the level architecture and furnishing placements to get past patrolling guards

Stage 3: Prototype development

When all designs and contents got confirmed after a series of group meeting, I, as a game developer, produced first prototype with C# and Javascript in unity game engine.

Stage 4: Design refinements

With the completion of the prototype, it helped us answer a few key questions prior to the completion of prototype.Is a cooperative stealth game viable, will different view and map brings conflicts and ruin the cooperation between two players? We found at the end of prototype that the answers to all questions is a yes. So, we split screen and allow user to share same map in order to increase their communication and integrate character models,narrative and animation into our game, add two new levels with two new environments, make cameras turn back on, and further enhance the current diegetic UI components.

Decision Trees

Refined game world

For our first level. Both players are split off and will traverse through a short section of a level by themselves. As for narrative the players get a taste of what’s happening in the outside world and what they are tasked to do. Our second level has the two player meet up and begin the second half of the tutorial. Here, the players get a taste of working together to successfully progress through the level.

For our second level, both players are just past the beginning of it all and are trying to locate the Head office. Here, the players should find information about the workplace and whatever secrets they may find within this organization.

Stage 5: Beta version

The team agrees that the game requires more polish, art direction. the kinesthetic of our game need to be improved so that the game is more enjoyable to play.The AI and UI will also be further refined to ensure that the game and the UI feedback feels like a complete package. So, we have decided to include a way-pointer or compass bar at the top that will indicate to the lpayer the general direction of their main objective, we also create energy bar for different player to indicate the skill using situation.
